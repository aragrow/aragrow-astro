AI Usage Policy – Aragrow, LLC

By David Arago · October 14, 2025

At Aragrow, LLC, we use Artificial Intelligence (AI) as a practical tool—just as a carpenter uses a table saw, skill saw, or drill—to improve productivity and reduce costs for our clients. As a technology company, we are committed to leveraging available tools responsibly for your benefit.

  • Transparency: We will always inform you at the start of our engagement that we use AI, as well as how and where it may be applied throughout your project.
  • Respect for Preferences: If you prefer that no AI tools be used in your project, we ask that you share this with us. We will honor your request and discuss alternative solutions.
  • Protection of Sensitive Information: At no point will we use AI in connection with your sensitive information. We take all reasonable precautions to ensure your private data stays confidential and secure.
  • Privacy Commitment: We make every effort to ensure your personal and private information remains private, regardless of the technology in use.

If you have questions, concerns, or specific requirements regarding the use of AI in your project, please let contact us at davidarago@aragrow.me. We want you to feel confident and comfortable working with Aragrow, LLC.
