ProductCopy Genie: Automated Product Content Creation for WordPress Store Owners

By David Arago · December 30, 2024

The ProductCopy Genie plugin is a personal learning project designed to help me delve deeper into Python and Artificial Intelligence. It allows users to create perfectly tailored resumes and cover letters for job applications, leveraging advanced AI technologies like Google Gemini, ChatGPT, Anthropic Claude, and locally hosted Ollama.

Save Time, Sell More, and Scale Your Store!

Running a small online store requires juggling many tasks, and creating high-quality product descriptions can eat up hours every day. With ProductCopy Genie, you can automatically generate engaging and SEO-friendly product descriptions, alt text, and post content with just a few clicks—allowing you to focus on growing your business instead of spending time on repetitive content writing.

Powered by Google Gemini API, this plugin takes your product images and turns them into compelling copy that’s ready to sell. Whether you're managing hundreds of products or just a handful, ProductCopy Genie helps you streamline your content creation process and improve your store's SEO—all while saving you valuable time.

Key Benefits:

  • Save Several Hours a Day: No more writing product descriptions manually. Let the plugin handle it, so you can focus on inventory management and customer engagement.
  • Increase Sales: With automatic, high-quality product descriptions, alt text, and SEO-friendly content, you’re set to attract more customers and boost conversions.
  • Manage More Inventory: Focus on scaling your store, not writing endless content. Automatically generate descriptions for every product—quickly adding new inventory without the extra effort.
  • SEO-Optimized Content: Improve your visibility on search engines with AI-generated product copy that’s designed to rank.
  • Increased Accessibility: Automatically generated alt text ensures that your images are accessible to all users, including those with visual impairments.
  • Global Reach: With automatic multi-language post creation, you can easily serve customers in multiple regions without needing to write translations yourself.
  • Improved User Experience: Product descriptions and content generated by AI can be more consistent and comprehensive, ensuring customers always get clear, detailed information about your products.
  • Reduced Overhead: By automating the content creation process, you can focus more on managing your store, interacting with customers, and scaling your business.

Let ProductCopy Genie do the heavy lifting so you can grow your store faster, with less effort!

Technology Stack

  • WordPress: Core platform for plugin integration and content management.
  • Python: Backend processing for AI queries and data management.
  • Google Gemini: Advanced AI model powering expert-level responses.
  • MongoDB (Planned): Future releases may integrate MongoDB to store user interaction history, enabling personalized learning experiences.

How ProductCopy Genie Works: A Step-by-Step Process

1. Uploading the Image to WordPress

When you upload an image to your WordPress site (whether it's a product image or a media file for a post), the plugin automatically detects the new upload.

  • Where: Media Library in WordPress.
  • What: Any image you upload (e.g., product images, promotional graphics).
  • Why: This is the starting point for generating content automatically based on the image.

2. Sending the Image to Google Gemini API

Once the image is uploaded, our plugin sends the image to the Google Gemini API. This advanced API processes the image and returns the following:

  • Captions: A brief description of the image, perfect for the Alt Text field.
  • Alt Text: Automatically generated alternative text that describes the image for accessibility purposes (SEO and user experience).
  • Image Description: A longer, more detailed description of what the image represents, which can be used as a product or post description.

3. Attaching the Image to a Post

After the image is uploaded and processed by Google Gemini, you can then attach the image to a post or a product page. This step allows you to choose how you want to use the generated content.

  • Where: On the post or product edit page in WordPress.
  • What: You will see options for using the generated content or writing your own.
  • Why: The generated captions, alt text, and descriptions are ready for use, but you can always edit them to suit your needs.

4. Choose Content Creation Method

As the store editor or manager, you can choose between two options:

  • Option 1: Manually Writing Content: If you prefer to write the product title, description, and other content yourself, you can enter that information directly.
  • Option 2: Automated Content Generation: If you'd like to save time, you can let the plugin automatically generate the post or product content for you. Based on the image description and the category list you've selected, the plugin will send this data to the Google Gemini API to generate:
    • Post Title: A catchy, SEO-friendly title for the post or product page.
    • Post Content: A detailed, relevant description of the product or content based on the image and the category selected.
    • Excerpt: A brief summary or teaser text to show in the post or product preview.
    • Category Selection: The plugin will suggest the most appropriate category for your post or product based on the content generated by the Google Gemini API.

5. Multi-Language Content Creation

If you have a multi-language store, the plugin can also handle the creation of translated versions of the post.

  • What: The plugin can automatically translate the generated post content into multiple languages.
  • How: It uses the Google Gemini API to generate content in the languages you choose, helping you reach a global audience.
  • Why: This is ideal for store owners with international customers who need content in multiple languages but don’t have the time or resources to manually translate.

Real-World Use Cases

  • E-Commerce Stores: Automatically generate product descriptions, alt text for images, and multi-language posts for your product catalog.
  • Bloggers: If you run a content-based store (like a blog), you can use this tool to automatically generate blog post content and summaries based on images and categories.
  • Retailers with Limited Time: If you're a busy store owner with a lot of products and little time to write individual product descriptions, this tool makes it easy to generate content automatically.

Getting Started with the Plugin

  1. Install the Plugin: After downloading, install the plugin in your WordPress site like any other plugin.
  2. Activate the Plugin: Once installed, activate the plugin from the WordPress dashboard.
  3. Set Up API Key: Enter your Google Gemini API credentials into the plugin settings.
  4. Upload Your Images: Begin uploading your product images to the Media Library.
  5. Select Content Generation Option: On the product/post edit screen, choose whether you want to generate content automatically or write your own.
  6. Review & Publish: Review the generated content and make any necessary adjustments before publishing your post or product page.

Conclusion

With ProductCopy Genie you can automate a major part of the content creation process, making it easier to maintain a well-populated online store without spending hours writing product descriptions, alt text, and post content. Whether you want to save time, improve SEO, or serve a global audience, this solution can help you achieve your business goals with minimal effort.

By integrating the Google Gemini API into your workflow, you can focus on growing your business while the plugin handles the heavy lifting of content generation.
