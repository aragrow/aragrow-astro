<p>Like many websites, Aragrow, LLC (“we,” “our,” or “us”) uses “cookies” exclusively to enhance your experience with our Site by allowing you to pick up your previous conversation where you left off.</p>

<p><strong>What are cookies?</strong><br>A cookie is a small piece of data stored on your computer by your web browser when you visit a website. Each time you return, your browser shares this data with our servers to help us provide a seamless, personalized experience.</p>

<h2 class="wp-block-heading">Types of Cookies We Use</h2>

<p>We use only one category of cookies:</p>

<ul class="wp-block-list"><li><strong>Persistent Cookies:</strong> These cookies help you retrieve your latest LetDavidAssist Chat conversation when you return to our Site. They allow us to keep your session history accessible for your convenience.</li>
</ul>

<p><strong>Note:</strong><br>We do&nbsp;<strong>not</strong>&nbsp;use cookies for analytics, advertising, user engagement, tag management, affiliate programs, blog comments, or any other third-party tracking or service. Our cookies are strictly limited to conversation retrieval functionality.</p>

<h2 class="wp-block-heading">Control Over Cookies</h2>

<p>You can choose whether to accept cookies by adjusting your browser settings. For information on how to clear or block cookies, please see&nbsp;<a rel="noreferrer noopener" target="_blank" href="https://www.aboutcookies.org/">https://www.aboutcookies.org</a>. Declining cookies may prevent you from retrieving your previous session history and may limit certain functionalities of our Site.</p>

<p>For further questions or concerns about our Cookie Policy, please contact us at [your contact email].</p>

<hr class="wp-block-separator has-alpha-channel-opacity"/>

<p><strong>This policy confirms that Aragrow, LLC does not use cookies for any marketing, analytics, or advertising purpose—only for the legitimate function of user conversation retrieval.</strong></p>
