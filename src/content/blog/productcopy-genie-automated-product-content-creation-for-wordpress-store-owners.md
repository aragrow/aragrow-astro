<p>Powered by <strong>Google Gemini API</strong>, this plugin takes your product images and turns them into compelling copy that’s ready to sell. Whether you're managing hundreds of products or just a handful, <strong>ProductCopy Genie</strong> helps you streamline your content creation process and improve your store's SEO—all while saving you valuable time.</p>

<h2 class="wp-block-heading">How ProductCopy Genie Works: A Step-by-Step Process</h2>

<h3 class="wp-block-heading">1. Uploading the Image to WordPress</h3>

<p>When you upload an image to your WordPress site (whether it's a product image or a media file for a post), the plugin automatically detects the new upload.</p>

<ul class="wp-block-list"><li><strong>Where:</strong> Media Library in WordPress.</li>

<li><strong>What:</strong> Any image you upload (e.g., product images, promotional graphics).</li>

<li><strong>Why:</strong> This is the starting point for generating content automatically based on the image.</li>
</ul>

<h3 class="wp-block-heading">2. Sending the Image to Google Gemini API</h3>

<p>Once the image is uploaded, our plugin sends the image to the <strong>Google Gemini API</strong>. This advanced API processes the image and returns the following:</p>

<ul class="wp-block-list"><li><strong>Captions</strong>: A brief description of the image, perfect for the <strong>Alt Text</strong> field.</li>

<li><strong>Alt Text</strong>: Automatically generated alternative text that describes the image for accessibility purposes (SEO and user experience).</li>

<li><strong>Image Description</strong>: A longer, more detailed description of what the image represents, which can be used as a product or post description.</li>
</ul>

<h3 class="wp-block-heading">3. Attaching the Image to a Post</h3>

<p>After the image is uploaded and processed by Google Gemini, you can then <strong>attach the image to a post</strong> or a product page. This step allows you to choose how you want to use the generated content.</p>

<ul class="wp-block-list"><li><strong>Where:</strong> On the post or product edit page in WordPress.</li>

<li><strong>What:</strong> You will see options for using the generated content or writing your own.</li>

<li><strong>Why:</strong> The generated captions, alt text, and descriptions are ready for use, but you can always edit them to suit your needs.</li>
</ul>

<h3 class="wp-block-heading">4. Choose Content Creation Method</h3>

<p>As the store editor or manager, you can choose between two options:</p>

<ul class="wp-block-list"><li><strong>Option 1: Manually Writing Content</strong>: If you prefer to write the product title, description, and other content yourself, you can enter that information directly.</li>

<li><strong>Option 2: Automated Content Generation</strong>: If you'd like to save time, you can let the plugin <strong>automatically generate</strong> the post or product content for you. Based on the image description and the <strong>category list</strong> you've selected, the plugin will send this data to the Google Gemini API to generate:<ul class="wp-block-list"><li><strong>Post Title</strong>: A catchy, SEO-friendly title for the post or product page.</li>

<li><strong>Post Content</strong>: A detailed, relevant description of the product or content based on the image and the category selected.</li>

<li><strong>Excerpt</strong>: A brief summary or teaser text to show in the post or product preview.</li>

<li><strong>Category Selection</strong>: The plugin will suggest the most appropriate category for your post or product based on the content generated by the Google Gemini API.</li>
</ul>
</li>
</ul>

<h3 class="wp-block-heading">5. Multi-Language Content Creation</h3>

<p>If you have a multi-language store, the plugin can also handle the creation of translated versions of the post.</p>

<ul class="wp-block-list"><li><strong>What:</strong> The plugin can automatically translate the generated post content into multiple languages.</li>

<li><strong>How:</strong> It uses the Google Gemini API to generate content in the languages you choose, helping you reach a global audience.</li>

<li><strong>Why:</strong> This is ideal for store owners with international customers who need content in multiple languages but don’t have the time or resources to manually translate.</li>
</ul>

<h2 class="wp-block-heading">Getting Started with the Plugin</h2>

<ol class="wp-block-list"><li><strong>Install the Plugin</strong>: After downloading, install the plugin in your WordPress site like any other plugin.</li>

<li><strong>Activate the Plugin</strong>: Once installed, activate the plugin from the WordPress dashboard.</li>

<li><strong>Set Up API Key</strong>: Enter your Google Gemini API credentials into the plugin settings.</li>

<li><strong>Upload Your Images</strong>: Begin uploading your product images to the Media Library.</li>

<li><strong>Select Content Generation Option</strong>: On the product/post edit screen, choose whether you want to generate content automatically or write your own.</li>

<li><strong>Review &amp; Publish</strong>: Review the generated content and make any necessary adjustments before publishing your post or product page.</li>
</ol>
