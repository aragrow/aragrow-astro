<p>At Aragrow, LLC, we use Artificial Intelligence (AI) as a practical tool—just as a carpenter uses a table saw, skill saw, or drill—to improve productivity and reduce costs for our clients. As a technology company, we are committed to leveraging available tools responsibly for your benefit.</p>

<ul class="wp-block-list"><li><strong>Transparency:</strong> We will always inform you at the start of our engagement that we use AI, as well as how and where it may be applied throughout your project.</li>

<li><strong>Respect for Preferences:</strong> If you prefer that no AI tools be used in your project, we ask that you share this with us. We will honor your request and discuss alternative solutions.</li>

<li><strong>Protection of Sensitive Information:</strong> At no point will we use AI in connection with your sensitive information. We take all reasonable precautions to ensure your private data stays confidential and secure.</li>

<li><strong>Privacy Commitment:</strong> We make every effort to ensure your personal and private information remains private, regardless of the technology in use.</li>
</ul>

<p>If you have questions, concerns, or specific requirements regarding the use of AI in your project, please let contact us at <a href="mailto:davidarago@aragrow.me">davidarago@aragrow.me</a>. We want you to feel confident and comfortable working with Aragrow, LLC.</p>
